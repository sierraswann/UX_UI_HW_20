Wander Woman

Year: 2020
Duration: 2 Weeks
Sierra Swann | Chelsea Robinson

The Problem: Women who travel are constantly worried about safety and expenses when they travel domestically and abroad.
They want to find a safe place to stay that is away from touristy areas so they can have a more authentic, local experience.
           
           The Solution: To make female traveling experiences more enjoyable, we intend to develop an app that will offer women
real-time emergency assistance. The app will also alert them to local, female-owned establishments and offer a chat room
for connections in country.
          
           My Role: UX Researcher, UX/UI Designer
          
           Tools: Adobe XD, Google Suite, Miro, Zoom, Google Forms, Otter
